Top three places to visit in Hyderabad

The Secretariat of Hyderabad

The Telangana new secretariat is situated in the heart of Hyderabad, the capital city of Telangana. It is located near the Hussain sagar lake.

The Statue of equality

The Statue of Equality is a statue of the 11th-century Indian philosopher Ramanuja. The Statue of Equality is the second tallest sitting statue in the world. The base building hosts a vedic digital library, research centre, ancient Indian texts, theatre and a gallery. Ramanuja's works are presented in the gallery.

The Golconda fort

The fortress rests on a granite hill 120 meters high while huge crenellated ramparts surround this structure. Golconda still boasts of mounted cannons, four drawbridges, eight gateways, and majestic halls, magazines, stables etc
